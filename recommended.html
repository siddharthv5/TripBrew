Taj Mahal

Location: Agra, India
Standing on the banks of River Yamuna, the Taj Mahal is indeed a marvelous structure. The name "Taj Mahal" was derived from the name of Shah Jahan's wife, Mumtaz Mahal, and means "Crown Palace".The quality and beauty of this untarnished white marble, the amazing designs, exquisite decorations, precious gemstones, and its stunning location make this an unforgettable vist that you should not miss.
Time taken for visit: 2-3 hours
Best time to visit: Sunrise hours (6:00/6:30), Sunset hours (7:00/7:30), but it is beautiful during the day as well.
CLOSED ON FRIDAYS WITH THE EXCEPTION OF MUSLIM PRAYERS.

Agra Fort

Location: Agra, India
This incredible fort isn't a far off distance from the wonderful Taj Mahal, and it is truly an amazing fusion of Mughal and Hindu culture. The strong walls of the fort guard the beautiful artwork in the inside, and the many incredible sights to see will take you back in time, and make you feel exactly like you are visiting royalty in 16th century India.
Time taken for visit: 2-3 hours
Best time to visit: It is a sight to visit at any time.
OPENS AT SUNRISE, CLOSES AT SUNSET.

Jama Masjid

Location: Agra, India

The Jama Masjid is another magnficent structure that was built by Shah Jahan, and it is a mosque that is truly worth visiting. Of places to visit in Agra, the Jama Masjid is a magnificent structure.The verses of the Holy Quran were recited before consecrating and laying down of each stone, and you can see all of the hard work put in to making this wonderful structure. There are two tall minarets flanking the mosque,and the architecture here is breathtaking. This is a sight you must see when you do stop by Agra.
Time taken for visit: 1-2 hours
Best time to visit: You should visit this sight in the mid-morning or evening time.
OPENING TIME 6AM, CLOSING TIME 8PM.

Akbar's Tomb

Location: Agra, India
This tomb is another wonderful masterpiece from the Mughal period. Akbar commisioned this construction himself, but it was finished by his son Jahangir. It is surrounded by very high walls, and the top of the tomb is made of marble. This top is yet a false tomb, which is a marble pavilion. This structure has both the white marble of the Taj, and the red sandstone of the Agra Fort. It is must see place!
Time taken for visit: 1-2 hours
Best time to visit:You can visit this structure any time of the day, but preferrably not in the mid-day heat.
OPENING TIME 6AM, CLOSING TIME 6PM..

Moti Masjid

Location: Agra, India
This is another structure, called the Pearl Mosque that was made by Shah Jahan. It shines brightly when the sun falls upon it due to the beautiful white marble. There is a gorgeous sundial that is inside this monument, and there are quite a few beautiful prayer halls in this location.It is located upon the River Yamuna as well, and the view from this mosque is enthralling.
Time taken for visit: 1-2 hours
Best time to visit:Visit this monument during the early sunlight hours to see the marble shining.
OPENING TIME 5:30AM, CLOSING TIME 6PM..

Fahtepur Sikhri

Location: Agra, India
This is a whole city that was built by the ruler Akbar, and was the capital of the Mughal Empire in India for quite a long period of time. Akbar commissioned the wonderful garderns,buildings, and the monuments in this early city, and just taking a stroll through you will shoot you back to the past.
Time taken for visit: 1-2 hours
Best time to visit:You can visit this city any time of the day.
OPENING TIME 9:00 AM, CLOSING TIME 5:00 PM.

Jodhabai's Palace

Location: Agra, India
This was the famous palace of Akbar's famous queens. It is a simple building, that is quite beautiful as well. Inside the palace, there are quite a few slits in the wall, which you can see the Taj Mahal through. The design includes Gujrathi and Rajasthani styles. There is also hints of Hindu architecture.This is the palace of one of Akbar’s queens who was his favourite. Also called Jodha Bai’s Palace it is a simple yet sophisticated building.It is quite the architectural wonder of the Mughal area. It is located within the city of Fahtepur Sihkri.
Time taken for visit: 1-2 hours
Best time to visit:You can visit this city any time of the day.
OPENING TIME 9:00 AM, CLOSING TIME 5:00 PM.

Sikandra Fort

Location: Agra, India
The very tomb of Akbar the Great is located inside this magnficent fort. It is made of red sandstone, which was a prominent material at this time,and is designed beautifully on the outside and the inside.There is extremely rich artistry in the area, and it is a truly breathtaking place to visit.
Time taken for visit: 30mins-1hr (without Tomb)
Best time to visitVisit this monument anytime of the day, and you should follow it with a visit to Akbar's tomb.
OPENING TIME 5:30AM, CLOSING TIME 6PM..
